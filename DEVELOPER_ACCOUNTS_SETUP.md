# Developer Account Setup Guide

## Overview

To publish LifeZinc to app stores, you need:
- **Google Play Console** - $25 one-time (Android apps)
- **Apple Developer Program** - $99/year (iOS apps)

---

## Google Play Console Setup

### Cost
**$25 USD one-time registration fee** (never expires)

### Prerequisites
- Google Account (Gmail)
- Credit/Debit card
- Developer information
- $25 registration fee

### Step-by-Step Registration

#### 1. Go to Google Play Console
Visit: https://play.google.com/console/signup

#### 2. Sign in with Google Account
- Use existing Gmail or create new one
- Recommended: Create business-specific account (e.g., dev@lifezinc.com)

#### 3. Accept Developer Agreement
- Read the Google Play Developer Distribution Agreement
- Check the box to accept
- Click "Continue to payment"

#### 4. Pay Registration Fee
- Enter credit/debit card information
- Pay $25 USD (one-time, lifetime access)
- Payment processes immediately

#### 5. Complete Account Details

**Account Type: Choose One**

**Personal Account** (Individual):
- Your name
- Address
- Phone number
- Use if: Solo developer, no business entity

**Organization Account** (Business):
- Business name: LifeZinc LLC (or your company name)
- Business address
- Business phone
- D-U-N-S number (optional but recommended)
- Use if: Registered business, multiple developers

**Recommendation for LifeZinc:**
Start with Personal, upgrade to Organization later if needed.

#### 6. Developer Profile Information

Fill out:
- **Developer name** (public-facing): "LifeZinc" or your name
- **Email address**: For user support (public)
- **Phone number**: For Google to contact you
- **Website**: Your LifeZinc website URL (optional but recommended)

#### 7. Verify Phone Number
- Google sends verification code
- Enter code to verify

#### 8. Complete Identity Verification
Google requires verification (anti-fraud):
- Upload government-issued ID
- Provide additional information if requested
- Processing: 1-3 days usually

#### 9. Set Up Payment Profile (Optional)
Only needed if selling paid apps or in-app purchases:
- Add merchant account
- Provide tax information
- Bank account for payments

**For LifeZinc:** Skip if free app, set up later if adding paid features.

### After Approval

You'll receive email confirmation when approved (usually within 24-48 hours).

**Next Steps:**
- Access Google Play Console
- Create your first app
- Start testing and publishing

### Google Play Console Features

Once approved, you can:
- Publish unlimited apps
- Create internal/beta test tracks
- View analytics and crash reports
- Respond to user reviews
- Manage app releases
- Set pricing and distribution

## Apple Developer Program Setup

### Cost
**$99 USD per year** (auto-renews annually)

### Prerequisites
- Apple ID
- Credit/debit card
- Mac computer (for app building)
- Xcode (free from Mac App Store)
- Developer information
- $99/year membership fee

### Step-by-Step Registration

#### 1. Create/Use Apple ID
Visit: https://appleid.apple.com

- Use existing Apple ID or create new one
- Recommended: Use business email if possible
- Enable two-factor authentication (required)

#### 2. Enroll in Apple Developer Program
Visit: https://developer.apple.com/programs/enroll/

Click "Start Your Enrollment"

#### 3. Sign In
- Sign in with your Apple ID
- Verify two-factor authentication code

#### 4. Choose Entity Type

**Individual/Sole Proprietor:**
- Your legal name
- Uses your personal identity
- Faster approval (usually same day)
- Apps published under your name

**Organization/Company:**
- Business legal name
- Requires D-U-N-S number
- Requires business verification
- Apps published under company name
- Takes 2-7 days for approval

**Recommendation for LifeZinc:**
- Individual: Faster, good for MVP launch
- Organization: More professional, better long-term

#### 5. Complete Personal Information

**For Individual:**
- Legal name (as on government ID)
- Address
- Phone number
- Birthdate

**For Organization:**
- Legal entity name
- D-U-N-S number (get free from Dun & Bradstreet)
- Business address
- Business phone
- Your role (e.g., CEO, CTO, Founder)
- Legal authority to bind organization

#### 6. Accept License Agreement
- Read Apple Developer Program License Agreement
- Agree to terms
- Continue

#### 7. Purchase Membership
- Review enrollment information
- Click "Purchase"
- Pay $99 USD
- Payment via credit/debit card or Apple Pay

#### 8. Verification Process

**Individual:**
- Usually instant to few hours
- May require ID verification

**Organization:**
- Apple verifies business legitimacy
- D-U-N-S number checked
- May contact via phone
- Takes 2-7 business days

#### 9. Confirmation
- Receive email when approved
- Access to developer resources activated
- Can now submit apps

### Getting D-U-N-S Number (for Organizations)

**What is D-U-N-S?**
- Unique 9-digit business identifier
- Required for organization enrollment
- Free from Dun & Bradstreet

**How to Get:**
1. Visit: https://developer.apple.com/enroll/duns-lookup/
2. Search for your business
3. If not found, request new D-U-N-S number
4. Free process, takes 5-10 business days
5. Apple checks against this for verification

### After Approval

**You Get Access To:**
- App Store Connect (publish apps)
- Xcode beta versions
- Technical support
- Developer forums
- Certificates and provisioning
- Analytics and reports
- TestFlight for beta testing

### Important Notes

**Renewal:**
- Auto-renews yearly at $99
- Manage in Apple Developer account settings
- Cancel anytime (apps removed after expiration)

**Mac Requirement:**
- Need Mac computer to build iOS apps
- Xcode only runs on macOS
- Alternatives: Rent Mac in cloud, use PWABuilder

**App Review:**
- Apple manually reviews every app
- Takes 24-48 hours typically
- Stricter than Google Play
- Must follow App Store Review Guidelines

## Tips for Success

### General
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication everywhere
- Keep developer account information secure
- Use professional email addresses
- Respond quickly to verification requests

### Google Play
- Complete developer profile fully (looks professional)
- Consider adding business address even as individual
- Set up email forwarding for support emails
- Review policies before first submission

### Apple
- Mac is required for traditional iOS development
- PWABuilder can generate iOS package without Mac initially
- Budget for annual renewal
- Learn App Store Review Guidelines
- Join developer forums for help

### Both Platforms
- Read all terms and conditions
- Keep payment information updated
- Monitor email for important notices
- Update account info when business changes
- Maintain compliance with policies

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Google Play

**Issue: Identity verification taking too long**
- Solution: Check email for requests, respond promptly
- Typically resolves in 1-3 days

**Issue: Payment declined**
- Solution: Verify card has international payments enabled
- Try different card or PayPal

### Apple

**Issue: D-U-N-S verification failing**
- Solution: Ensure business info matches legal records exactly
- Contact Apple Developer Support

**Issue: Organization not found**
- Solution: Request new D-U-N-S number, wait 5-10 days

**Issue: Two-factor authentication problems**
- Solution: Update trusted devices, contact Apple Support
