# LifeZinc Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 24, 2024**

## Introduction

Welcome to LifeZinc. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the LifeZinc mobile application.

**LifeZinc** is a mental wellness companion app that helps you transform difficult emotions into healing through journaling, mood tracking, and spiritual support.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**
- Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
- Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
- Profile information (optional: name, preferences)

**Journal & Wellness Data:**
- Journal entries and reflections
- Mood tracking data and emotional patterns
- Tags and categories you create
- Voice recordings (if you use voice journaling)
- Goals and challenges you set
- Faith preferences and spiritual content selections
- Therapy export data

**Usage Data:**
- App features you use
- Time spent in app
- Pages visited within the app
- Settings and preferences

### Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- Browser type and version
- IP address
- General location (country/region, not precise location)

**Analytics Data:**
- App performance metrics
- Error reports and crash logs
- Feature usage statistics

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### Provide Core Services
- Create and maintain your account
- Store and sync your journal entries across devices
- Provide mood tracking and emotional insights
- Deliver personalized spiritual support
- Enable therapy tools and features
- Generate AI-powered reframing and encouragement

### Improve Your Experience
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Develop new features based on user needs
- Provide customer support

### Communication
- Send important updates about the app
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Notify you about new features (if you opt in)
- Send security alerts if necessary

### Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Protect against fraud and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## How We Store Your Data

### Your Private Database
- Your personal data is stored in a **private Supabase database** dedicated to your account
- Journal entries and wellness data are **NOT** shared with other users
- Each user has isolated, secure data storage

### Security Measures
We protect your data using:
- **Encryption in transit** (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Encryption at rest** (database encryption)
- **Secure authentication** (industry-standard protocols)
- **Row Level Security** (RLS) policies on database
- **Regular security audits**
- **Access controls** (only you can access your data)

### Data Retention
- **Active accounts**: Data retained indefinitely while account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: All personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Journal entries**: Retained until you delete them or delete your account
- **Analytics**: Anonymized analytics may be retained longer for improvement purposes

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT Sell Your Data
**LifeZinc will never sell your personal information to third parties.**

### Limited Data Sharing

We only share data in these specific circumstances:

**Service Providers:**
- **Supabase** (database hosting) - stores your data securely
- **OpenAI** (if using AI features) - processes journal entries for insights (anonymized)
- Service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements

**Legal Requirements:**
We may disclose information if required to:
- Comply with legal process (subpoena, court order)
- Protect our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or security issues
- Protect user safety

**Business Transfer:**
If LifeZinc is acquired or merged, your data may transfer to the new entity. You'll be notified and can delete your account before transfer.

### We Never Share
- Your journal entries with other users
- Your personal information with advertisers
- Your data with social media platforms
- Your wellness data with insurance companies or employers

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control
You have the right to:
- **Access** your data at any time through the app
- **Export** your journal entries and data
- **Edit** or update your information
- **Delete** specific journal entries or your entire account
- **Opt out** of optional data collection

### How to Exercise Your Rights

**View Your Data:**
- Access all your data through the app's profile and journal sections

**Export Your Data:**
- Go to Settings > Export Data
- Download your journal entries in JSON or text format

**Delete Your Account:**
- Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
- All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

**Update Preferences:**
- Go to Settings > Privacy
- Adjust notification, analytics, and data sharing preferences

**Contact Us:**
- Email: privacy@lifezinc.com (replace with your actual email)
- We'll respond within 30 days

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you're a California resident, you have additional rights:
- Right to know what data we collect
- Right to delete your data
- Right to opt out of data sales (note: we don't sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Contact us to exercise these rights.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR:
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification (correct inaccurate data)
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

Contact us or use in-app settings to exercise these rights.
